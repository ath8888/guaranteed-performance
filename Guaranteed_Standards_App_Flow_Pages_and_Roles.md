## 🗺 `app-flow-pages-and-roles.md`

### 🧭 Site Map (Top-Level Pages)

- **Set Your Standard** (onboarding)
- **Home**
- **Plan**
- **Check-in**
- **Guarantee**

---

### 🧩 Purpose of Each Page

|Page|Purpose|
|---|---|
|**Set Your Standard**|Choose the required test(s), enter baseline and deadline, confirm the Guarantee|
|**Home**|Show plain progress facts and today's session at a glance|
|**Plan**|Show this week's fixed sessions (5/3/1 + BBB, or the adapted version for pushups/running)|
|**Check-in**|Log a real attempt at the Standard; view simple history|
|**Guarantee**|Recap terms, baseline vs. current vs. target, and the refund request action|

---

### 👥 User Roles & Access Levels

|Role|Description|
|---|---|
|**Solo User**|Full access to all features; no login, no account|

> 🛑 No sign-up, no cloud account, no roles beyond the device — by design, for MVP.

---

### 🚶 Primary User Journeys (Max 3 Steps Each)

#### 🎯 Set a Standard

1. Open the app for the first time, tap **Set Your Standard**
2. Choose the test(s), enter baseline and deadline
3. Confirm the Guarantee — Plan is generated automatically

---

#### 🏋️ Complete a Plan Session

1. Open **Home**, see today's session listed
2. Tap into **Plan**, follow the fixed session
3. Mark complete — Home updates automatically

---

#### ✅ Log a Check-in

1. Tap **Check-in** from Home
2. Enter the real result (time, reps, or weight)
3. History and Home update immediately — no derived score, just the new fact

---

#### 🤝 Request a Refund

1. After the deadline, open **Guarantee**
2. Review the terms and result recap
3. Tap **Request Refund** — confirmation explains payment processing is pending (Phase 2)
